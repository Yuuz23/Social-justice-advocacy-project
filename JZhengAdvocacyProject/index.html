classic Movie

"The Hate U Give"

"The Hate U Give" is a powerful drama about a young black girl who witnesses the fatal shooting of her friend by a white police officer, and becomes an activist in the fight for justice and racial equality.

"HARRIET"

"Harriet" is a biographical film that tells the story of Harriet Tubman, an iconic abolitionist who escaped slavery and helped lead hundreds to freedom through the Underground Railroad.

"FRUITVALE STATION"

"Fruitvale Station" is a gripping drama based on the true story of Oscar Grant, a young black man who was fatally shot by a white police officer, and offers a commentary on race relations and police brutality in America.

"JUST MERCY"

"Just Mercy" is a biographical drama film based on Bryan Stevenson's memoir. It follows his efforts to exonerate Walter McMillian, who was wrongly convicted and sentenced to death. The film tackles issues of racial injustice and highlights the importance of compassion.

"12 YEARS A SLAVE"

"12 Years a Slave" is a historical drama film based on the true story of Solomon Northup, a free black man who was kidnapped and sold into slavery. The movie portrays his struggles and explores themes of racism and oppression.

"THE HELP"

"The Help" is a drama film based on a novel about African-American maids in Mississippi during the Civil Rights era. The movie follows a young white woman who writes a book about their experiences with the help of two maids. The film explores themes of racial inequality and community.
